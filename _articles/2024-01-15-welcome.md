## How to Use This Template

### Daily Workflow:

1. **Create a new folder** for today's date in `assets/images/articles/`:
   ```
   assets/images/articles/2024-01-16/
   ```

2. **Add your images** to that folder:
   - `featured.jpg` (main headline image)
   - Any content images

3. **Create a new article file** in `_articles/`:
   ```
   _articles/2024-01-16-your-article-title.md
   ```

4. **Copy this format** and fill in your content:

```markdown
---
layout: article
title: "Your Article Title Here"
date: 2024-01-16
author: Your Name
featured_image: /assets/images/articles/2024-01-16/featured.jpg
---

Short excerpt that appears on the homepage.

<!-- more -->

Full article content goes here...
```

5. **Commit and push** to GitHub - your new article will appear automatically!

### Tips:
- Keep featured images around 1200x600px for best results
- Compress images before adding (use tools like TinyPNG)
- The site builds automatically when you push to GitHub

Enjoy creating your magazine! 📰✨
